About TypeState

TypeState is a research initiative designed to quantify the correlation between cognitive load (stress) and fine-motor control (keystroke dynamics).

By analyzing micro-fluctuations in flight time (the time between keys) and dwell time (how long a key is pressed), we aim to build adaptive interfaces that can detect user frustration in real-time.

Privacy
